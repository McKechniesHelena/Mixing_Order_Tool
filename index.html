    A.P.P.L.E.S. order

    1. 1 Soluble powders SG, SP, dry fertilizer (incl. AMS)
    2. 2 Dry powders DF, WDG, WP
    3. 3 Liquid flowables / suspensions SC, SE, ME, F, L, CS, ZC, DC
    4. 4 Emulsifiable concentrates EC, EW, OD (incl. oil adjuvants)
    5. 5 Solutions S, SL (incl. surfactants)

    Agitate throughout. Each component must be fully mixed before the next. Within a group, add the pesticide before the adjuvant.
